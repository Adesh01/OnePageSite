When will you announce concerts?
Who's gonna play! We get it, everyone wants to hear about the amazing upcoming concerts and when they go on sale. We plan to announce the first line-up of shows in the next few months and we can't wait to share it - so keep an eye on the website and SIGN UP to receive our email newsletters so you're one of the first to know!
Do you have a VIP Program / Premium Seating?
If you are looking to take your concert experience to the next level, we will have a variety of killer VIP options for you. Our concert membership program includes access to the VIP lounge Foundation Room and will provide many in-venue concert benefits, member's only perks and advanced ticketing options. Want to learn more? Contact our FOUNDATION ROOM team and we'll get right back to you.
How do I buy tickets to upcoming shows when they go on sale?
Once tickets go on sale there are many ways to purchase tickets for concerts and events House of Blues. Tickets will be available on ticketmaster.com, livenation.com and you also buy tickets in person at the House of Blues box office once we open later this year.
How can I rent the House of Blues for a private party?
Our new venue is ideal for private events of all kinds. We will have multiple spaces and can cater to groups of 25-3,000 for special events. Our hospitality specialists are ready to make your event rock and help take it to the next level. Please contact katiepederson@livenation.com for more information and we'll start the planning!
What kind of food will you serve?
Great food is at the heart of what we do and you'll discover a variety of tastes at House of Blues restaurants. Our main restaurant is called Crossroads and you'll enjoy authentic dishes like Jambalaya and Voodoo Shrimp plus some of the tastiest soulful and classic comfort cuisine in town. From big, bountiful salads and sandwiches to signature burgers, steaks and ribs our menu has a little something for everyone. Our VIP lounge Foundation Room will feature a more upscale menu and top-tier drinks offering guests a chance to indulge a little before, during and after the show. Didn't get a chance to eat before heading into the music hall to see the show? Don't worry we've got you covered there too with a tasty menu that's easy to eat on the go.
Will there be parking?
There is tons of parking available at GardenWalk. Fans can valet or self-park at their convenience.
How do I get a job at House of Blues?
Keep an eye out for our announcement about a Job Fair for those interested in joining the House of Blues team. You can also always check livenationcareers.com for current open positions and we will update that site as soon as jobs are announced.
